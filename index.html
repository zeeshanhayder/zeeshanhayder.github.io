Dr. Zeeshan Hayder - Computer Researcher

Dr. Zeeshan Hayder

Research Scientist

I am a Research Scientist in the Imaging and Computer Vision Group (ICVG) at Data61/CSIRO and an Honorary Lecturer in the College of Engineering, Computing and Cybernetics (CECC) at ANU, specializing in Computer Vision and Machine Learning. My research interests include vision-language models, scene graph generation, algorithms for scene understanding (such as object detection, semantic segmentation, pose estimation, tracking and action localization) and representation learning via self-supervised, weakly-supervised and incremental learning.
